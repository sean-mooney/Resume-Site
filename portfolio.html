CPMOONEY.COM

Using SquareSpace, this website was built for my father who is a Construction Project Manager. The goal of this website was to provide a platform for him to show off his impressive portfolio and list homes he currently had available for purchase. While SquareSpace provided an easier development process, it had many limitations that required workarounds to perfect the product. One example of a limitation was the lack of pagination for a gallery; the page would begin to slow down because he had too many pictures that had to be on one page. To get around this, I added a previous and next button, a row of numbers representing pages, and wrote a script to determine which page should be displayed based on the current page, and highlight the current page's number. It was a good demonstration of hacking around the limitations of a software to achieve a better result.
